SCHEMES AND LAWS

Housing and Land Security

  • Housing Tenure Act for Agricultural Labourers: This act grants ownership rights to land cultivated by agricultural laborers for over five years, promoting stability and empowering them to break the poverty cycle.
  • Adi Dravidar Free Concrete Housing Scheme: This scheme provides free, high-quality housing to marginalized communities, significantly improving living conditions.

Education and Empowerment

  • Free Compulsory and Elementary Education Act (RTE): This central government law ensures free and quality education for children aged 6-14, benefiting marginalized communities.
  • Tamil Nadu Scholarship Schemes: The state government offers various scholarships for Scheduled Castes (SCs), Scheduled Tribes (STs), and Other Backward Classes (OBCs) to promote higher education access.
  • Reservation in Educational Institutions: The state upholds reservation policies in government colleges and universities for SCs, STs, and OBCs, ensuring equitable access to higher education.

Economic Upliftment and Livelihood

  • Ambedkar Development Corporation: This corporation provides financial assistance for SC entrepreneurs to establish businesses and create self-employment opportunities.
  • Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development of Backward Classes: This corporation supports OBC communities with loans, subsidies, and skill development programs for economic advancement.
  • Indira Gandhi Mattu Abhivriddhi Yojana: This central government scheme empowers tribal communities by supporting the development of Self-Help Groups (SHGs) for income generation.

Social Protection and Justice

  • Prevention of Atrocities Act: This act safeguards SCs and STs from caste-based violence and discrimination, ensuring their safety and dignity.
  • Free Legal Aid Services: The government provides legal assistance to marginalized communities to navigate legal issues, promoting access to justice.

Additionally

  • Reservation in Government Jobs: The state upholds reservation policies in public sector jobs for SCs, STs, and OBCs, ensuring fair representation in government employment.
  • Healthcare Initiatives: The government implements various healthcare programs for marginalized communities, providing subsidized medical care and promoting well-being.
